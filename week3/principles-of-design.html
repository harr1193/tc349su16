Principles of Design Assignment

Signal-to-Noise Ratio

This is a screenshot of Apples website, it is an example of signal-to-noise ratio because the layout and color of the design is kept simple. It is clear and easy to navigate around the page for vistors with minimal ads.

Hick's Law

This is a screenshot of a coffee brewing company's site. This is an example of Hick's Law because the menus are kept very clear and it is easy to navigate to where you need to go, whether that be purchasing coffee, or contacting them. There aren't many different decisions to be made on the main page.

80/20 Rule

This is a screenshot of adding a new address to the Amazon site to ship your order to. This is an example of the 80/20 rule because the majority of Amazon orders are shipped to the United States currently, so the country option for the address is defaulted to the United States. This is focused on benefitting whatever place is recieving the highest percentage of Amazon orders.

Modularity

This is a screenshot of a graphic designers webiste showcasing some of their work. This is an example of modularity because the site is combining photos of the work they are showcasing to make of the entire page of the website.

Chunking

This is a screenshot of the bottom of a news sites page. This is an example of chunking because is grouping articles split by type in chunks to display more information that is easier for visitors to naviagate and process.
