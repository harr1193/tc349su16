Principles of Design Assignment

Signal-to-Noise Ratio

This is a screenshot of apples website which I chose to represent the signal-to-noise ratio design principle because it is kept very simple and not much thinking is needed to get to where you need to go. The buttons at the top of the page make it clear where to click for what you are looking for and the design of the site is kept simple from a color and layout perspective.

Hick's Law

This is a screenshot of a coffee brewing company selling it's differnt kinds of coffee. This site represents hick's law because its menus and navigation are kept very simple. There aren't many decisions to make to get to where you need to go as you can tell from the menu buttons.

80/20 Rule

This is a screenshot of Amazon where you need to enter a new address for your orders to be shipped. This is representative of the 80/20 rule because the majority of the location where Amazon orders are shipped are in the United States and that country is selected by default in the country optiion box. The selection is catered to where a large percentage of where the people who purchase items ship.

Modularity

This is a screenshot fo a designers website with some of their work displayed on the main pain. This is an example of modualrity because each picture is combined to make up the design of the website and how the user views the content.

Chunking

This is a screenshot of the bottom of a newsite page where its navigations to different categories of news are placed. This is an example of chunking because it is a logical way to group many difffernt articles of news by location in a pleasing way.
